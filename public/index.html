Just another youtube downloader

How to use

Queries

url: youtube video url or id

info: true or false

type: video, audio or both

quality: low or high (Doesn't work with type=both nor type=audio)

Examples

Get video info: youtube?info=true&url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

Get video in low quality: youtube?info=false&url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ&type=video&quality=low

Get video in high quality (No audio): youtube?info=false&url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ&type=video&quality=high

Get video with high quality and audio: youtube?info=false&url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ&type=both

Get only audio: youtube?info=false&url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ&type=audio
